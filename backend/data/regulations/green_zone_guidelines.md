# Green Zone Guidelines - Indian Metropolitan Areas

## Purpose

This document outlines the operational guidelines for commercial vehicles operating within designated Green Zones in major Indian metropolitan areas.

## Designated Green Zones

### Delhi NCR

1. **Connaught Place Core** - 2km radius from Rajiv Chowk
2. **India Gate Complex** - 1km radius
3. **Chandni Chowk Heritage** - Entire market area
4. **Hospital Zone** - AIIMS, Safdarjung, RML (500m each)

### Mumbai

1. **CST Heritage Zone** - 1km radius
2. **Marine Drive** - Full stretch
3. **Bandra Business District** - BKC area
4. **Gateway of India** - 500m radius

### Other Cities

Similar zones designated in Bengaluru, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, and Pune.

## Compliance Requirements

### Entry Requirements

- Valid BS-VI certification
- Active GPS tracking
- Emission monitoring device
- Green Zone permit (daily/monthly)

### Operating Restrictions

| Parameter       | Standard Zone | Green Zone    |
| --------------- | ------------- | ------------- |
| Max Idle        | 90 seconds    | 60 seconds    |
| Speed Limit     | As posted     | 30 km/h       |
| Operating Hours | 24/7          | 6AM-10PM only |
| Horn Usage      | Restricted    | Prohibited    |

### Penalty Structure

| Violation       | First Offense | Repeat Offense    |
| --------------- | ------------- | ----------------- |
| Excess Idle     | ₹2,000        | ₹5,000            |
| Speed Violation | ₹1,000        | ₹3,000            |
| No Permit       | ₹10,000       | ₹25,000 + Impound |
| Emission Spike  | ₹5,000        | ₹15,000           |

## Real-Time Monitoring

Fleet operators must:

1. Report entry/exit timestamps
2. Transmit continuous emission data
3. Respond to alerts within 5 minutes
4. Maintain communication with drivers

## Emergency Exceptions

Green Zone restrictions may be relaxed for:

- Medical emergencies
- Fire services
- Law enforcement
- Authorized municipal services
